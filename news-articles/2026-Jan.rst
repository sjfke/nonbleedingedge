Display and Searching
=====================

To display click the date ``DD.MM.YYYY`` title.

At the moment, ``site search`` will point out the pages of interest, which are organized ``per month`` to keep the numer of files
needed to a reasonable number. The ``contents`` are collapsed so manually searching in your browser will search all the
articles in that month. *A better solution is needed.*
